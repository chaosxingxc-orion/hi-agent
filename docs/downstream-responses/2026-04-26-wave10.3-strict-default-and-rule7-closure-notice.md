## 1. Scope and posture

Wave 10.3 closes three platform-contract classes that blocked Wave 10.2 from the 75+ readiness band.

**Class 1 — Strict-posture wiring (W3-A):** HumanGate, OpHandle, and GateStore were warn-only under research/prod posture. Wave 10.3 wires `posture_guards.require_tenant` to all three, making empty-tenant a hard `ValueError` under research/prod. Unscoped GateStore reads raise instead of warning. This is the largest single contributor to the 安全与租户隔离 dimension delta.

**Class 2 — Rule 7 alarms (W3-B):** `runner.py` finalize and `get_fallback_events` paths degraded silently. The http_gateway FailoverChain had no Prometheus counter, no WARNING log with run_id, and no `fallback_events` entry. Wave 10.3 adds all four Rule 7 components (Countable + Attributable + Inspectable + Gate-asserted) to each identified silent-degradation site.

**Class 3 — Governance and verification (W3-C/D/E):** `check_select_completeness.py`, `check_doc_consistency.py`, and `check_t3_freshness.py` governance gates were advisory; W3-C promotes them to blocking. W3-D refreshes the delivery governance scripts. W3-E (this track) delivers `scripts/verify_clean_env.py` (Windows-safe basetemp wrapper), capability matrix refresh, and this draft notice.
